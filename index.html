the leader

Saman is an engineer with great appreciation for computer arts. He holds a B.Sc in computer software engineering from Sharif University of Technology, Tehran, Iran. In 2016, he received his MFA in Design + Technology from Parsons School of Design, New York. In his professional life he focuses on web development, creative software development, and human computer interaction. He is also a computer programming instructor. His artworks often use robotics, sensory environments and physical computing as the means for communicating his ideas. Currently, as part time faculty at Parsons School of Design, he teaches Web Advanced: JavaScript and Physical Computing Advanced.
